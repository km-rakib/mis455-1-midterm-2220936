Frequently Asked Questions

What's your shipping policy?

We offer free shipping on all orders over $50. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. We ship Monday-Wednesday to ensure plants don't sit in transit over the weekend.

What if my plant arrives damaged?

Contact us within 24 hours with photos of the damaged plant, and we'll send a replacement or issue a refund at no additional cost to you.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only ship within the continental United States due to agricultural import regulations.

Can I pick up my order in-store?

Yes! Select "Store Pickup" at checkout, and we'll notify you when your order is ready for collection.
